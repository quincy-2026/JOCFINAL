Esper Hand

The Esper Hand is a bionic prosthetic hand.
It uses AI to enhance the functionality of a prosthetic hand.
The AI can predict and analyze patterns of intended movement faster and more efficiently.
The Esper Hand also uses an electrimyography brain-computer interface to trigger movement and gather brain activity.
